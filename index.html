What is CapCut?

CapCut is a free video editing app developed by ByteDance, the same company that owns TikTok. It allows users to trim clips, add music, apply filters, insert text, and even use AI tools — all in one app.

Whether you’re a beginner or a professional, CapCut makes editing easy and enjoyable. It’s the perfect companion for TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Instagram Reels creators.
